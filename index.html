Our Story

At Take a Break, We believe in creating moments of joy through exceptional coffee and homemade treats. We blend specialty coffee with literary comfort. Enjoy our rotating book collection alongside handcrafted drinks.

Artisan Coffee

Small-batch roasted beans sourced from ethical farms worldwide.

Homemade Goodness

Fresh pastries baked daily using traditional recipes.

Community Hub

A welcoming space for coffee lovers since 2011.

Free High-Speed WiFi

Work or relax with our complimentary high-speed internet.

Reading Corner

Browse and borrow from our curated collection of books, novels, magazines, and art books while enjoying your drink.

(Available for in-cafe reading)
